Charity

Charity For Gentlemen was established in 1982. In London, England. The first store opened, is the same store as the current store, located in London. The goals have always been to serve people with beautiful clothing and at the same time help the once in need. It has never been about making a profit on the sales, but to donate the profits to the people who need it more than the owners of Charity For Gentlemen. All of the profits are sent to the charity organisations on the last day of every month. .
